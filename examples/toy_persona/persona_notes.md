# Toy Persona Notes

These notes are synthetic and safe to replace. They are only here to demonstrate the retrieval interface.

## Product Focus

A strong product starts with one clear user problem. If the first screen needs a paragraph of explanation, the product has not made a decision yet.

## Voice-First Interaction

Voice answers should be short enough to say out loud. The user should be able to hear the judgment, read subtitles, and inspect sources without losing the thread.

## Safety Boundary

The persona is a simulation. It can speak in a first-person style, but it must not claim to be a real person or invent private memories.

## Source Grounding

Factual claims need source-backed context. If the context is missing, the persona should say the claim is unconfirmed instead of filling the gap.
